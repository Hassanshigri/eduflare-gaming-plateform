Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Eduflare, we collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, book coaching sessions, or interact with our platform. This includes your name, email address, phone number, gaming preferences, and payment information when you purchase our services.

We also automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our platform, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, access times, and pages viewed. This information helps us improve our services and provide a better user experience.

When you participate in coaching sessions, we may collect gameplay data, performance metrics, and session recordings to help our coaches provide personalized feedback and track your progress over time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our gaming coaching services. This includes matching you with appropriate coaches, scheduling sessions, processing payments, and providing customer support.

Your information helps us personalize your experience on our platform, recommend relevant coaches and training programs, and send you important updates about your account and our services.

We may also use your information for research and analytics purposes to understand how our platform is used and to develop new features and services that benefit our gaming community.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this privacy policy. We may share your information with our coaches to facilitate coaching sessions and provide personalized training.

We may also share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, conducting our business, or serving our users, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

We may disclose your information if required by law, to protect our rights or property, or to ensure the safety of our users and the public.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data, secure server infrastructure, and regular security audits.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. You can also contact us directly to request changes to your information or to exercise your privacy rights.

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications or by contacting us directly.

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to object to certain processing activities.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities and to provide personalized content and advertisements. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

For more detailed information about our use of cookies, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your continued use of our services after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@eduflare.org or +1 555-123-4567. We are committed to addressing your concerns and protecting your privacy rights.
