Best CS2 Crosshair Settings for 2025

By Coach Sarah
• February 10, 2025 • 8 min read
← Back to Blog

A well-optimized crosshair can significantly improve your performance in Counter-Strike 2. This guide explores the best crosshair settings used by professional players in 2025 to help you enhance your aim and accuracy.

Why Crosshair Settings Matter

Your crosshair is your primary visual guide for aiming. A good crosshair should be visible, non-distracting, and tailored to your playstyle. Understanding how to customize size, color, and style is key to consistent performance.

Key Crosshair Settings

1. Size and Thickness

Smaller crosshairs are ideal for precision aiming, while thicker crosshairs improve visibility. Pros often use a size of 1-2 and thickness of 0.5-1 for a balance between accuracy and clarity.

2. Color

Choose a color that contrasts with common map backgrounds, such as green, cyan, or white. Avoid colors like red or brown, which may blend into environments.

3. Gap and Outline

A small negative gap (-1 to -3) keeps the crosshair compact, while an outline (thickness 1) enhances visibility against bright backgrounds. Experiment to find your preference.

Pro Player Crosshair Codes

Below are example crosshair codes from top CS2 professionals. Copy these into your console to test them out.

s1mple’s Crosshair

cl_crosshairsize 1; cl_crosshairgap -2; cl_crosshairthickness 0.5; cl_crosshaircolor 1;

ZywOo’s Crosshair

cl_crosshairsize 2; cl_crosshairgap -3; cl_crosshairthickness 1; cl_crosshaircolor 4;

How to Customize Your Crosshair

Use the CS2 settings menu or console commands to adjust your crosshair. Practice in deathmatch servers to test new settings and ensure they suit your playstyle.

Conclusion

Optimizing your crosshair is a simple yet effective way to improve your CS2 performance. Experiment with pro settings, adjust based on your preferences, and practice consistently to see results.
