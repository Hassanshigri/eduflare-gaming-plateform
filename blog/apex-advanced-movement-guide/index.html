Master Advanced Movement in Apex Legends

By Coach Emma
• April 12, 2025 • 9 min read
← Back to Blog

Advanced movement techniques in Apex Legends can give you a significant edge over opponents. This guide covers wall-bouncing, slide-jumping, and other mechanics to help you outplay in 2025.

Why Movement Matters

Apex Legends is a fast-paced battle royale where movement is key to survival. Mastering advanced techniques allows you to evade shots, close gaps, and reposition effectively.

Core Movement Techniques

1. Slide-Jumping

Slide-jumping maintains momentum while moving across the map. Sprint, slide, and jump in quick succession to cover ground faster and avoid enemy fire.

2. Wall-Bouncing

Jump toward a wall, slide briefly, and jump again to redirect your momentum. This technique is ideal for confusing enemies during fights.

3. Tap-Strafing

Change direction mid-air by flicking your movement keys and mouse. Practice in the firing range to master this high-skill technique.

Legend-Specific Movement

Legends like Octane and Pathfinder have unique movement abilities. Learn to combine their kits with general mechanics for maximum mobility.

Practice Drills

Use the firing range or custom lobbies to practice these techniques. Focus on consistency and incorporate them into your gameplay gradually.

Conclusion

Mastering movement in Apex Legends requires practice and patience. Integrate these techniques into your playstyle to gain a competitive advantage.
